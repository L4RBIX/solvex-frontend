# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Serious competitive programmers on Codeforces (Pupil → Master) who train deliberately and want to know *why* their rating is stuck. They arrive skeptical, data-literate, and allergic to hype. Secondary: coaches and study groups running private leaderboards and duels.

## Product Purpose

SolveX turns a user's public Codeforces history (submissions, verdicts, tags, rating ranges, retry patterns) into a friction-area diagnosis, a rating comfort zone, and a focused 7-day training queue. Gamification (XP, streaks, quests), private leaderboards, and 1v1 Arena duels keep training sustained. Success = a user trusts the diagnosis enough to follow the queue for a week.

SolveX is NOT an official Codeforces product. It uses public Codeforces data only and never claims affiliation.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, honest, engineered. A premium intelligence tool for ambitious programmers — Apple.com / pleep.app clarity with a Codeforces-native edge. Confident but never hyped; every claim on the page must be true of the product today (features not shipped are labeled "Soon").

## Anti-references

- Cheap gamer UI: RGB glows everywhere, badge spam, aggressive gradients.
- Toy CP websites: dense tables, default Bootstrap, clutter.
- Fake-claims SaaS: invented user counts, "AI-powered" hand-waving, testimonials that don't exist.
- Vercel-template look: default favicon, stock glyphs (◈ ◉ ◆) as icons.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence over decoration** — show real verdicts, tags, and numbers in mockups; the product's data IS the visual.
2. **Dark, calm, precise** — near-black green (#020806) surfaces, one neon green accent (#00F5A0), generous spacing, sharp typography (Rebond Grotesque headings, Inter body).
3. **Subtle motion, always optional** — fade/rise entrances with ease-out-quint, everything gated by `prefers-reduced-motion`.
4. **Honesty is the brand** — "no login required", "public data only", "not affiliated with Codeforces" stated plainly; unshipped features marked Soon.
5. **Premium restraint** — fewer borders, more space; no glyph clip-art, no fake stats, no shouting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on body text, visible keyboard focus rings, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all animation, 44px touch targets on mobile, semantic HTML and ARIA labels on interactive elements.
